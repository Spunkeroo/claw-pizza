Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

claw.pizza ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at claw.pizza and use our claw machine game services.

Summary: We collect minimal data needed to run the game. We never sell your personal information. We use localStorage for game state and Firebase for real-time features.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: If you connect a Bitcoin wallet to claim prizes, we store your public wallet address. We never have access to your private keys.
  • Display Name: If you set a username for the leaderboard, we store that name.
  • Contact Information: If you reach out to us via email or social media, we retain the information you provide in your communication.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Activity: We record plays, wins, claims, and game interactions to maintain leaderboards and ensure provably fair outcomes.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type for optimizing the game experience.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referral source, and click patterns to improve our service.
  • IP Address: Collected for rate limiting, fraud prevention, and basic analytics. We do not use IP addresses for advertising or tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Game Operation: To run claw machine games, process prize claims, maintain balances, and display leaderboards.
  • Provably Fair Verification: To generate and store cryptographic seeds that let you verify every game outcome is fair.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent abuse, multi-accounting, and manipulation of game outcomes.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve game performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Communications: To send prize claim notifications, service updates, and (if opted in) daily faucet reminders via push notifications.

3. Cookies & localStorage

We do not use traditional tracking cookies. Instead, we use the following browser storage mechanisms:

  • localStorage: To store your session ID, game state, token balance, preferences, and provably fair seeds. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to third-party advertisers.
  • IndexedDB: To enable offline play functionality. Queued plays and claims are stored locally and synced when connectivity is restored.
  • Service Worker Cache: To cache game assets for faster loading and offline access.

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings, but doing so will reset your game session and local balance.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process data:

Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Real-time database for leaderboards, game state synchronization, and user authentication.
  • Data shared: Anonymized player IDs, game results, wallet addresses (for prize claims).
  • Privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Stripe

  • Purpose: Payment processing for optional token purchases.
  • Data shared: Payment information is sent directly to Stripe and never touches our servers. We only receive a confirmation of payment.
  • Privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

Amazon Associates

  • Purpose: Affiliate links for physical prizes (e.g., pizza-related merchandise).
  • Data shared: When you click an Amazon affiliate link, Amazon may set cookies on your device to attribute purchases. We receive only anonymized purchase reports.
  • Privacy policy: Amazon Privacy Notice

GitHub Pages

  • Purpose: Website hosting.
  • Data shared: GitHub may collect IP addresses and request metadata as part of standard web hosting.
  • Privacy policy: GitHub Privacy Statement

5. Data Retention

  • Game Data: Play history, wins, and leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely to maintain historical leaderboards and verify provably fair outcomes.
  • Wallet Addresses: Retained as long as your account is active or until you request deletion.
  • IP Addresses: Retained for up to 90 days for fraud prevention, then permanently deleted or anonymized.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics are retained indefinitely. Individual session data is retained for up to 12 months.
  • Offline Sync Data: Stored locally on your device until successfully synced, then removed from local storage.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data. Note that this will remove your leaderboard entries and game history.
  • Portability: You can request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of push notifications at any time through your browser settings. You can clear localStorage to remove all locally stored data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 7 below. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Firebase Security Rules to restrict database access.
  • No storage of private keys or sensitive financial data on our servers.
  • Rate limiting and abuse detection systems.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

claw.pizza is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Continued use of claw.pizza after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
